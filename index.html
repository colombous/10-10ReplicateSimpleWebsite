About Us

International Poverty Foundation is a non profit organization that helps poor Middle Eastern and South Asian countries by providing education, food and medical treatments.Since 2005, we have helped over 200 countries, saving 353 million people from brink of death. With your help, we can save even more people. Join today to help people with needs or donate to be a hero.
